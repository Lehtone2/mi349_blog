My Daily Routine as a Student

September 25, 2025 | By Jacob

Waking Up:

I wake up and then check my phone and make sure I didn't miss anything important. I check my classes on D2L and make sure I have nothing that I have to do immediately, in this order:

  • MI349
    • Week (current week)
    • Assignment Due Dates
  • MI250
    • Lab
    • In Class Assignment
  • ISS210
    • Week (current week)
    • Assignment Due Dates
  • MI201
    • Week (current week)
    • Assignment Due Dates
  • MI355
    • Week (current week)
    • Assignment Due Dates

After making sure I'm not missing assignments, I grab some breakfast and check my work schedule. If all is clear, I brush my teeth and hop on my PC to start the day.

My Routine Schedule:

Here's a breakdown of what a typical day looks like for me:

Time Activity Notes
7-8 AM Wake up & check classes D2L assignments and deadlines
9 AM Breakfast If homework due → start early; if not → PC gaming
3-4 PM Go for a walk No matter what, I get outside
6-7 PM Dinner Relax with food and reset
8 PM-Midnight Work or Gaming Finish homework or unwind with friends

Evenings:

My evenings are usually about winding down. After dinner, I'll check once more if anything new popped up for class or work. Then, I might watch a show or hang out with friends online. I always try to leave at least thirty minutes before bed for relaxing away from screens. It helps me get a better night's sleep and reset for the next day. Even though I don't sleep very well.

The Benefits of Walks

September 27, 2025

Walks are one of the simplest yet most effective ways to improve overall heatlh. Fresh air and light exercise help wake the body, especailly in the morining, thus energizing the mind, and preparing your for the day. Unlike high-intensity workouts, walking is accessible to almost everyone, requiring no equiptment or special training.

Beyond physical health, morning walks promote mental well being. Walking in nature reduce stress, improves mood, and enhances focus. Studies suggest that even 20 minutes of walking daily can lead to significant long term benefits. Personally, I've a decent amount of improvement in my productivity whenever I stick to this habit.
